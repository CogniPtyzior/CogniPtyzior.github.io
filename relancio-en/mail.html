Relancio

Email

Configure POP3/IMAP reception, SMTP sending, Gmail and attachments.

POP3 / IMAP reception

Relancio uses a reception account that you control. You choose the reception mode, server, port, security, username and password.

  • POP3 is often suitable for simple retrieval.
  • IMAP may be preferable if the provider or your workflow relies on a synchronized mailbox.
  • The Test connection button lets you verify the settings before use.
  • Retrieval can be automatic or manual with the Retrieve button.
  • The automatic retrieval frequency can be configured according to the desired rhythm.

SMTP sending

Sending goes through the SMTP server configured in the Sending tab. Relancio will send replies from this mailbox.

  • Enter the server, port, security, sending address, username and password.
  • Use the Send a test email button to verify the configuration before real use.
  • Replies are only sent after an explicit action on Send.

Gmail and email providers

Gmail can work with POP or IMAP depending on the chosen configuration. Exact settings depend on the provider. In many cases, Gmail requires an app password instead of the usual account password, especially when two-step verification is enabled.

Gmail POP example: server pop.gmail.com, port 995, security SSL/TLS, full username and app password.

Attachments

Attachments added to a reply are linked to the outgoing draft. Incoming or forwarded attachments, however, are not stored permanently or analyzed by Relancio.

Important limit: Relancio may display or use the names of incoming attachments as contextual information, but it does not read their content to generate the reply.
